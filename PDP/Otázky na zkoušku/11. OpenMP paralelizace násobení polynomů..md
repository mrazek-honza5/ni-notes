- Vstup:
	- Polynomy $A^m, B^n, a_m \ne 0, b_n \ne 0$ 
- Výstup
	- Polynom $C = A \times B = \sum^{m+n}_{k=0} c_k*x^k$ , kde $c_k = \sum^{min(k,m)}_{l=max(0, k-n)} a_l*b_{k-l}$
